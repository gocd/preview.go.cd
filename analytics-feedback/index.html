Feedback Topic: Analytics Plugin

If you are an existing GoCD user and interested in CD analytics, register today! We are looking for people to try our new analytics plugin and give us feedback.

What is GoCD Analytics Plugin? It's an enterprise plugin we build to provide insights into your pipelines. It helps you answer questions like:

  • How long do pipelines take?
  • How quickly do we recover from failures?
  • Which jobs are slow? Do we need to add more resources?

If you register and get qualified, we will start sending you a weekly gift throughout this 30-day program. Qualification for this program is based on how long and how extensively you have been using GoCD, so please include your GoCD usage information at your registration.

Along your feedback journey, we will have dedicated people from the GoCD team to help you get the plugin installed, answer your questions and listen to your feedback. You can use Slack or email to provide us feedback at your preferred cadence.

What you need to do:

  • Register here (we will let you know whether you are qualified within one business day via email)
  • Receive a link to download the latest GoCD Analytics plugin via email
  • Install and use the Analytics plugin for 30 days
  • Give us feedback along your journey via slack or email
  • Await your weekly gift!
More information:
  • This program is only open to current GoCD users , and participants will be selected based on how well your usage of GoCD matches our study criteria.
  • The registration is open now until June 1, 2019
  • After the program ends, the Analytics plugin is only available for paid GoCD Enterprise customers. Your license will expire, and you won’t be able to continue to use it unless you decide to move forward with an Enterprise contract.
